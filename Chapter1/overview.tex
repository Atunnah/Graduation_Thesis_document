\section{Motivation}
\subsection{Digitizing Vietnamese Documents}
Discusses the increasing demand for digitizing Vietnamese documents in administrative, educational, and archival contexts, and how OCR serves as a key enabler for this process.

\subsection{Challenges in Vietnamese OCR}
Highlights the unique difficulties of applying OCR to Vietnamese text, including complex diacritical marks, tonal variations, diverse fonts, and low-quality scanned documents.

\subsection{The Importance of Accurate Text Recognition in Vietnamese}
Argues why high recognition accuracy is critical, particularly for official documents, historical records, and information retrieval systems, and the consequences of recognition errors.

\section{Proposed Approach: Deep Learning-Based Vietnamese OCR}
\subsection{Approach}
Describes the high-level strategy of combining a transformer-based detector (RT-DETR) with a scene text recognizer (PARSeq) to build a robust Vietnamese OCR system.

\subsection{Pipeline Components and Workflow}
Provides a concise walkthrough of the end-to-end pipeline, from raw image input through text detection, cropping, and recognition to final text output.

\subsection{Model Optimization Strategy}
Briefly introduces the key optimization strategies applied to each stage, including fine-tuning and data augmentation for RT-DETR, and vocabulary expansion with linguistic constraints for PARSeq, highlihting why targeted optimization is central to this thesis's contribution.
